About Danny Limo Company

Danny Limo LLC was established in 2023 by Opeoluwa Olutola and takes great pride in offering outstanding service to a variety of clients throughout Chicago. We are prepared to go above and beyond your expectations, whether you're an executive with a corporate account, a group of friends on a tour, or just someone looking for opulent transportation in the city. .

Our drivers do more for us than just transport us from City to City. They are Professional chauffer who demonstrate excellent client's relationship to give you peace of mind while you travel. They are well-versed in the city's finest routes, ensuring that your ride is both luxury and effective.

Airport pick-up & drop off

Danny Limo LLC offers on-time service to Midway International Airport and O'Hare International Airport. Book your Black Car or Limousine service to ensure a worry-free pickup or drop-off.
